Microservice

Why you should learn Microservice

Learning microservices is crucial for software developers due to its benefits in building scalable, modular applications. With microservices, developers gain flexibility, independent deployment, fault isolation, and technology diversity. It enables faster development, scalability, and facilitates concurrent work on different services, resulting in more efficient and maintainable systems.

Book

The book "Monolith to Microservices" is an excellent resource for beginners learning about microservices. It provides a step-by-step guide on transitioning from a monolithic architecture to microservices, covering key concepts, principles, and practical strategies. With real-world examples and case studies, it helps developers understand the benefits, challenges, and best practices of microservices, enabling them to successfully implement and scale microservice architectures.

Monolith to Microservices: Evolutionary Patterns to Transform Your Monolith

Supplementary material

Laur Spilca's Software Architecture Fundamentals YouTube course is a concise and comprehensive resource for software developers seeking to learn about software architectures, including microservices. It covers essential topics such as architectural styles, design patterns, scalability, and maintainability, providing valuable insights to enhance developers' understanding and expertise in software architecture.
